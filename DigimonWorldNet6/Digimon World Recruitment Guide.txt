Recruitment Guide
Native Forest
Agumon
Level:         Rookie
Location:        Screen below the Green Gym.
How:         Enter the screen and win the inevitable fight.
Unlocks:        The Item Bank for storage of your inventory items.

Kunemon
Level:        Rookie
Location:        Screen left of the Forest toilet.
How:        Give it food and it will challenge you to a fight. Win the fight.
Unlocks:        Eat away the shrubbery between File City and the Digimon bridge.
Comment:        Makes the Knights who say nii very sad.

Palmon
Level:        Rookie
Location:        Screen below the Forest toilet.
How:        Talk to it 3 times to start a fight. Win the fight.
Unlocks:        Upgrades meat farm to yield giant meat, sells you giant meat for 500 bits each.

Coelamon
Level:        Champion
Location:        Second screen below Kunemon's bed when leaving the screen from the right.
How:        Go to Coela Point at dusk (16:00-19:59) and it will swim there. Talk to it and it will 
help you across the water. Now go up and across the newly repaired Digimon 
Bridge and go back to where you found Coelamon the first time and talk to it 
again.
Unlocks:        Shop or upgrades shop inventory.


________________


Seadramon
Level:        Champion
Location:        Southern Dragon Lake Island. 
How:        Go there between 08:00 and 08:59 or 17:00 and 17:59. Fish up the very long fish
swimming in the back of the lake. He swims from one side to the other and is not visible from time to time. Once caught, choose the friend dialogue.
Unlocks:        Ferries you between Beetle land and Dragon Eye Lake.
Comment:         Choosing the item during the dialogue gives you a Seadramon evolution item. 
The Technique dialogue gives you a new Ice technique. 

Etemon
Level:        Ultimate
Location:         Screen below Kunemon's Bed.
How:         At 50 pp or more you can interact with the door in the tree. Etemon does 
a flying knee drop on your Digimon for 30% of your Digimon’s max HP.(Will never kill). After that he fights you, win the fight.
Unlocks:        Sells Golden bananas, Etemon evolution item, for 50k bits, west of the Digimon
bridge.

Ninjamon
Level:         Champion
Location:         Path leading down at the File City side of the Digimon Bridge .
How:         Accept the fight and win.
Unlocks:         Provides general information on items when talking to him.
Beetle Land
Kuwagamon
Level:         Champion
Location:         Top Beetle Land screen, left training hall.
How:        Talk to Kuwagamon and choose the 2nd dialogue option.
Unlocks:        Improves MP, Offense and Brain training station at the Green Gym.



________________


Kabuterimon
Level:         Champion
Location:         Top Beetle Land screen, right training hall.
How:        Talk to Kabuterimon and choose the 2nd option.
Unlocks:        Improves HP, Defense and Speed training station at the Green Gym.
File City
Greymon
Level:         Champion
Location:         In front of Jijimon’s house.
How:         Have 15 pp or more when leaving Jijimon’s house. Greymon will 
challenge you, win the fight.
Unlocks:         The Arena
Comment:        You may use an Auto Pilot to skip walking out of Jijimon’s house and triggering 
this encounter.


Airdramon
Level:         Champion
Location:         In front of Jijimon’s house.
How:         Have 50 pp or more when leaving Jijimon’s house. Airdramon will attack 
you for 10% of your Digimon’s maximum health and start a fight. Win the fight. 
After beating Machinedramon he’ll have recovered from the fight and work at File 
City.
Unlocks:         “Fortune telling” in the Arena.
Comment:        You may use an auto pilot to skip walking out of Jijimon’s house and triggering 
this encounter.
Mt. Infinity
Devimon
Level:         Champion
Location:         To the right of the teleporter on the 4th floor of Mt. Infinity.
How:         Triggers upon moving east from the teleporter. He starts a fight, win the fight.
Unlocks:         Secret shop, sells DV chips.


Megadramon
Level:         Ultimate
Location:         Final floor of Mt. Infinity on a side-path.
How:         Choose to fight him, win the fight.
Unlocks:         Unknown.

MetalGreymon
Level:         Ultimate
Location:         Final floor of Mt. Infinity, take path upwards. 
How:         Win the fight.
Unlocks:        Comes up with nicknames for your Digimon.


Digitamamon
Level:         Ultimate
Location:         Final floor of Mt. Infinity after beating Machinedramon.
How:         Win the fight.
Unlocks:         Joins the restaurant. 
Comment:        Can keep on feeding your Digimon after it had 1 dish while hungry. This allows for 
payed stats gain. (Glitch)

Drill Tunnel
Drimogemon
Level:         Champion
Location:         Residential Area, screen left of the Drill Tunnel 2nd Floor, Underground Pond
How:         1. Talk to the Drimogemon in the Residential Area and win the fight
        2. Talk to the Drimogemon in  the Underground Pond.It will  takes 5 ingame days 
for him to dig the tunnel or until you have carried 10 carts of dirt to the entrance of 
the Drill tunnel. 
3. Once you’ve done the carrying leave the screen and come back again or 
come back after ingame 5 days have passed. Drimogemon will now be stuck on 
a big rock he can’t move. Any Champion or Ultimate Digimon is able to move the 
rock, opening up the path to Meramon. 
4. If you haven’t done so already recruit Meramon. You will find Drimogemon 3 
screens below Meramon in the Lava Cave or when entering the Lava Cave 2 
screens down in the Drill Tunnel. Talk Drimogemon and after 3 days he will have 
finished digging and opened up a path to Foot of Mt. Panorama. Talk to him or 
exit through to tunnel to trigger the recruitment dialogue.
Unlocks:         Treasure Hunter shop, Meramon's Cave, Path through Drill Tunnel to Gear 
Savanna
Comment:        Each time you carried dirt you will gain: 10 tiredness, 500 bits, 50 HP, 5 Off, 5 
Def


Meramon
Level:         Champion
Location:         Upon entering the Lava Cave straight up first screen.
How:         Win the fight against him.
Unlocks:         Restaurant, clears the rocks that block the path to Path thru Mt. Panorama
Comment:        To open up the Lava Cave complete the Drimogemon recruitment up until the 
point of pushing the big rock.
Gear Savanna
Elecmon
Level:         Rookie
Location:         Top left screen of the Gear Savanna where the main entrance to Factorial Town 
is.
How:         Talk 3 times to him.
Unlocks:         Powers the lights in the City.
Comment:        Your Digimon will be zapped for 20% max HP each time you talk to him, can’t kill.

Patamon
Level:         Rookie
Location:         Center top screen of the area, when exiting Factorial town from the main 
entrance first screen to the right.
How:         Win 3 times in a row against him.
Unlocks:         Shop or upgrades shop inventory.
Comment:        Stubborn little fellow.


Biyomon
Level:         Rookie
Location:         Screen south from Patamon
How:         Talk to Biyomon, she will run away, talk to her again. She will again run away. 
Walk about in the until a dialogue pops up that tells your digimon to fetch 
Biyomon. Depending on RNG Biyomon will run straight for you and be recruited. 
If she runs the wrong way repeat the previous.
Unlocks:         Shop or upgrades shop inventory.
Comment:        Sometimes the dialogue doesn’t seem to pop up or is hard to find. Talk to 
Biyomon to make her run away and try again. RNG reliant recruit, just keep
trying.


Sukamon

Leomon
Level:         Champion
Location:         1. Screen below Mt. Trash you'll find a Patamon chased by a Goburimon that

2. The screen left of that you find Leomon. Once you have the tablet talk to

Tropical Jungle


Vegiemon
Level:         Champion
Location:         Screen to the right of the toilet.
How:         1. After recruiting Palmon a Rain Plant Fruit will be ready to be picked during the 
15th day each month. You can find this fruit next to Tanemon. 
2. After picking it talk to Tanemon, now you can find a small plant in the target 
location. Interact with the plant and use the rain plant. Vegiemon will appear and 
ask a question, answer yes and he'll join the town.
Unlocks:         Upgrades the meat farm to yield Sirloin, sells you Supercarrot and Hawk Raddish

Centarumon
Level:         Champion
Location:        From the Digimon Bridge follow the coastline to the right until you reach the last 
screen, keep going up here until you reach  Amida Forest.
How:         You have to find the exit to the maze. It's an Amida kuji, so you have to switch 
lanes whenever possible. If you fail to switch the lane you'll get 50% of your max 
HP as damage. If you end up at the wrong exit you'll get teleported back and have 
to try again. Once you made it to the exit Centarumon will join the city and open a 
path to the Ancient Dino Region.
Comment:         You can find many valuable Items in the maze. What items you find seem to be 
RNG, the maze and the position of the exit however not. See the Amida Forest 
section for further explanation. (TL;DR move to the right instantly by holding right 
during the initial dialogue and you will be able to reach the end)
Unlocks:         The Clinic, here you may heal or rest your digimon (more effective than punimon) 
and buy bandages and medicine.

Tyrannomon
Level:         Champion
Location:         Ancient Dino Region, Ancient Glacial Region, next to the entrance to the Ancient 
Speedy Region.
How:         1. After recruiting Centarumon and entering the Ancient Dino Region you'll 
encounter him and have to win the fight against him. 
2.You will then meet him at the entrance to the Ancient Speedy Region, talk to 
him and enter the Ancient Speedy Region. Continue to the screen opposite of 
where you entered. You’ll end up in a screen which has a huge rib cage. There is 
a semi hidden passage on the left at approximately the last rib on the left. Keep 
hugging the left wall and you’ll enter the screen to the left. Here you’ll find 
Meteormon and fight him. After beating him go back and talk to Tyrannomon who 
will join the City.
Unlocks:         Restaurant

Bakemon

Vademon
Level:         Ultimate
Location:         Right Mt. Panorama Spore Area
How:        1. Go to his location, step on the marks on the floor, a textbox should appear. 
                       2. Read Shellmon's bulletin board (Entry 2), go back to the location and step on

                       3. When 45+ pp, read Shellmon's bulletin board (Entry 8), go back to the location

Misty Trees
In order to be able to navigate properly through Misty Trees you need to have talked to ShogunGekomon. To do that you have to find and fight Otamamon at the 2nd Geko Swamp screen when coming from Gear Savanna. He will insult you over and over. Eventually you will get a dialogue option stating “That gets me mad” which will result in a fight. Win this fight and you’ll be taken to ShogunGekomon. Beware, Otamamon is a random encounter.


Gabumon
Level:         Rookie
Location:         When you enter Misty Trees from Geko Swamp, exit the screen to the left and 
up. You will find Gabumon hiding behind a tree. (Agumon would never do that)
How:         Win the fight against him.
Unlocks:         Improves Treasure Hunter shop, increases chance to get a valuable item 
(evolution item/chip). More details in Treasure Hunter guide.


Kokatorimon
Level:         Champion
Location:         When you enter Misty Trees from Geko Swamp, exit the screen 2 times on the 
right. Kokatorimon will be there in the early morning between 4:00 and 6:59. 
(Purple time zone on the clock)
How:         Win the fight against him.
Unlocks:         A statue near the toilet.

How:         1. You need a Monzaemon as your partner Digimon. You can easily get one by

a Numemon.
                       2. Talk to the Tinmon in the Robot house (right), this will open up the Toy Mansion

3. In the Toy Mansion keep entering the doors marked with “ ? “ until you reach a 
floor with 2 boxes. Only 1 door will open and let you move up a floor, the other 
doors will drop an item which deals 10% max HP damage to your digimon. 
                       4. At the top floor you will fight WaruMonzaemon, win the fight and you’ll obtain a 
gear item which you will need to bring back to Tinmon in the Robot House.

Greylord Mansion
You need a Virus type Digimon to enter the Mansion.


Myotismon[a]
Level:         Ultimate
How:         1. Use the door on the right side of the stairs (you'll enter a room with a toilet), 
use the left door until you reach a large room with a fireplace. Interact with the 
fireplace and you'll find the mansion key.
                       2. Head back to the toilet room but chose the upper right door now. Walk along 
the south border of the screen until you find a spot to fall down through. (Your 
Digimon will receive 30% max HP damage)
                       3. Use the right door and interact with the coffin in the room to enter the hidden 
room where Myotismon is located. Talk to him and he will ask you to bring him 
meat and hands you a Fridge Key.
4. Go back to the screen with the 3 Soulmon and a fridge. (1 screen south from 
the toilet) Interact with the fridge. 
5. Leave the Villa and you'll find steak outside, pick it up.
6. Go back to Myotismon and talk to him.
7. When 35+ pp read Shellmon's bulletin board (Entry 6). 
8. Head back to the mansion and go to the place where you found Myotismon the 
first time and the dining room.
9. Attempt to leave the mansion
10. After Devimon disappears, use the first door on the right and use the 
elevator to go down to the Underground Lab. Stick to the left and keep going up 
until you go through a dark door opening. Keep going until you see a path to the 
right where you find Myotismon and talk to him. 
11. Go up to the next screen where you will find Skullgreymon. Win the fight 
against him. Skullgreymon will join the City
12. Go back to Myotismon and talk to him.
Unlock:         Fights in Arena


SkullGreymon
Level:         Ultimate
Where:        Underground Lab, final screen.
How:        Integrated in Myotismon’s recruitment.
Unlocks:        Fights in the Arena

Great Canyon[b]
You need to talk with Yuramon in File City after you unlocked the Digimon Bridge and he'll talk to you about an invisible bridge, after that you can head to the Great Canyon Entrance and use the invisible bridge. You can tell where it is from the odd 90 degree indentations into the ground.


Monochromon
Level:         Champion
Location:         The Great Canyon Top Area, the Shop next to the Toilet.
How:         When talking to him he'll let you work in his Shop to test if you're "worthy". If you 
manage to obtain over 3072 Bits profit he'll join the City.
Unlocks:         Shop
Comment:         The Shop Minigame is very RNG intensive and hard. Goburimon and Gotsumon 
are usually easy customers (you can often sell them expensive stuff, even if 
they're unhappy) while Muchomon and Weedmon have to treated carefully. This 
minigame costs you 8h of time and gives you a bit of money.

Birdramon
Level:         Champion
Location:         Starting at the invisible bridge keep exit the screen to the right. At the Y 
intersection follow the right path and exit the screen upwards. Keep going up until 
you reach an elevator and press UP. 
How:         Walk into the nest and Birdramon will appear. Win the fight against it
Unlocks:         Birdra Transport, the quick travel System of this game
Comment:         Once you've beaten Birdramon she'll take you back to File City

Shellmon
Level:         Champion
Location:        Great Canyon Bridge, use the elevator in Ogremon’s Fortress and follow the path 
leading to Freezeland.  
How:         1. After you've beaten Ogremon in his Fortress, use the elevator from the last 
room and follow the path until you hear someone calling for help. 
                       2. Head back to Ogre Fortress using the elevator you used to get up here 
3. Leave the Ogre Fortress through the Fortress Entrance screen.
4. Head right at the Fortress Entrance towards Great Canyon Bot.Area. Move 
upwards to the elevator, interact with it and hit Up.
5. Head to the left when coming out of the elevator to Freezeland.
6. Exit this screen to the left to reach the Great Canyon and talk to Shellmon.
Unlocks:         Shellmon’s Bulletin Board, contains some information as well as hints and trigger 
for certain recruitments.

Freezeland
Penguinmon
Level:         Rookie
Location:         Starting at the toilet screen exit to the top right and keep going right until you 
eventually reach Penguinmon standing in a door opening at the 4th screen.
How:         Talk with Penguinmon and he'll challenge you to a match of Curling. Win the 
game.
Unlocks:         Curling in the Arena, you need to pay a fish to play a game.
Comment:        If you lose you can play again, but you have to bring him a fish.


Moyjamon
Level:         Champion
Location:         Starting at the toilet screen exit to the top right and keep going right until you 
reach the 3rd screen. (Go right once more to reach Penguinmon’s location) Go 
down to the path that consists of ice chunks. There are 3 Mojyamon to be found 
that can be reached from this default location.
1. From the default location exit the screen to the right.
2. From the default location exit the screen to the bottom and keep right.
3. From the default location exit the screen to the bottom and keep left.
How:        Each Mojyamon offers you to trade 3 types of items and the last Mojyamon you all 
3 types of items with will join the City. 
You need the following Items: Digimushroom, Supercarrot (buyable at Vegiemon), 
Happymushroom (can be found in Native Forest sometimes) Giant Meat (can be 
bought at the Secret Shop in Gear Savanna), black trout (fishing), Hispeed Disk, 
Omnipotent, med. recovery and Digianchovy (fishing).
Unlocks:         Secret shop - sells Rest pillow (more sleep regen) and health shoes (HP+MP 
regen while walking)

Whamon
Level:         Champion
Location:        Starting at the toilet screen exit to the top right and keep going right until you 
reach the 3rd screen. (Go right once more to reach Penguinmon’s location) 
Keep to the left and head down to reach the path made from ice chunks, exit to 
the left. Stick to the right without exiting the screen and walk down the ice pier.
How:         1. Talk to Whamon and help him. He will take you to the Secret Beach Cave.
2. Exit to the right and fight Ogremon, WaruSeadramon and Gabumon. Win the 
fight against them.
Unlocks:         Fast travel to Factorial Town and the Secret Beach Cave. 
Comment:         You can find an evolution item for Digitamamon in the Secret Beach Cave.

Angemon
Level:         Champion
Location:        Ice Sanctuary. Starting at the toilet Screen exit to the top left, continue until you 
reach the Ice Sanctuary.
How:         1. Enter the Ice Sanctuary (only possible with a Vaccine type Digimon) and go to 
the statue until a dialogue appears. 
2. Move away from it and you Digimon will move it. A staircase appears from 
underneath the statue.
        3. Walk down the stairs.
4. Down the stairs, walk against the right wall and you'll find a secret entrance in 
the first small room.
5. Walk into the upper teleporter crystal.
6. At the 4 teleporter crystals walk into the upper right one
7. Win the fight against the 3 Digimon and retrieve the blue light.
8. Head all the way back to the statue at step 1.
Unlocks:         He'll give you hints about the Digimon you haven't recruited yet.


Frigimon
Level:         Champion
Location:        Starting at the toilet screen exit to the top right, walk here with your sick Digimon. 
To find Frigimon’s house starting at previously described location exit the screen 
up. Keep going left until you find Frigimon’s house.
How:         Take a sick Digimon from the list below to Freezeland and walk around a bit, there                 are zones you have to walk over to advance the quest.        An easy way to get your                 Digimon sick is to feed a Moldy Meat that can be bought from the Vending Machine in         the Ancient Dino Region. 
After you encountered Frigimon the first time evolve to a Digimon not on the list and                 talk to them again.
Who:        You need one of these Digimon to get sick: 
        Botamon, Koromon, Agumon, Greymon, Tyrannomon, Meramon, Punimon,                         Tsunomon, Kabuterimon, Birdramon, Poyomon, Tokomon, Kunemon, Yuramon,                 Tanemon, Monochromon, Kuwagamon
Unlocks:         Restaurant

Garurumon
Level:         Champion
Location:         Starting at the toilet screen exit to the top right. Exit to the top. Head left and exit 
to the bottom.
How:         Fight him and win the fight. He'll challenge you again for 4 p.m. the next day. If 
you miss that time you can still fight him, but he'll have more HP then.
        The 2nd fight has to be done without items or command, having a strong 
Digimon is recommended!
Unlocks:         Restaurant

Moving Recruitments
Nanimon
Level:         Champion (adds 1 pp)
Location:        1. Ogre Fortress, Ogremon’s location
2. Toy Town, WaruMonzaemon’s location
                       3. Speedy Dino Region - Meteormon’s location
                       4. Leomon's Cave - left of the tablet
                       5. Factorial Town Sewers - Numemon’s location
How:         Nanimon appears at 5 locations after certain recruitments are done.
                       1. After beating Ogremon in Ogremon’s Fortress as part of the Shellmon 
recruitment.
                       2. After Monzaemon’s recruitment.
                       3. After Tyrannomon’s recruitment
                       4. After Leomon’s recruitment
                       5. After Andromon’s recruitment
Unlocks:         1st time you find him he'll drop a keychain that increases your Inventory to 20 
slots. The 5th time you find him he'll drop a Key Chain for 30 slots.
Comment:         You can see him in the Item Bank, but there is no way to interact with him.


Ogremon
Level:         Champion
How:         1. Once you've visited the Great Canyon talk to Yuramon and he'll tell you
about bandits in the Great Canyon. Head back to the Great Canyon. After 
crossing the invisible bridge exit to the right and keep a right while moving up. 
You'll encounter and fight Ogremon and 2 Agumon here. Win the fight.
2. Go to the elevator and press DOWN. Exit the screen at the bottom. 
3. You'll find Ogre Fortress to the left. Enter it and in the last room you'll fight 
against Ogremon and 2 Agumon. Win the fight (Check Shellmon’s recruitment)
4. Go to Freezeland and find Whamon, see Whamon’s recruitment. At the 
Secret Beach Cave you will fight Ogremon, WaruSeadramon and Gabumon. 
Win the fight
5. Go to the Drill Tunnel and at Drill Tunnel 2nd Floor you'll fight Ogremon and 2 
Agumon again. Win the fight
Unlocks:         “Patrols” the City.
