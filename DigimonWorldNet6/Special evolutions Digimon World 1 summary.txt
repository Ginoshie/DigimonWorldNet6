Evolution:        Any In training ⇒ Kunemon
Condition:        Sleep in the area called Kunemon’s Bed.
Comment:        Save before sleeping, don’t sleep and save in order to retry on failure.
        50% chance of success.

Evolution:        Any digimon ⇒ Sukamon
Condition:        Full virus bar.
Comment:        The virus bar is full at 16 poops and carries over.
Rookie ⇒ Champion


Evolution:         Any rookie ⇒ Numemon
Condition:        Not eligible for evolution between 72h and 96h in rookie form.
Comment:        -20% current stats.

Evolution:        Any Rookie ⇒ Nanimon
Condition:        Scold while happiness = -100; discipline = 0.
Comment:        No evolution stats gain.


Evolution:        Any rookie(but Penguinmon) ⇒ Bakemon
Condition:        Lose a life (battle or illness)
Comment:        Store all items in the bank in order to prevent theft; Save before losing a life in order                 to retry on failure.
10% chance of success.

Evolution:        Shellmon ⇒ Coelamon (⇒ Megaseadramon) 
Condition:        Scold or praise during the 200th hour as Shellmon.
Comment:        Coelamon will digivolve to Megaseadramon as a result of battles, care mistakes 
reset, Coelamons target weight and a lack of evolution age reset.
